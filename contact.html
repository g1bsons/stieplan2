How long does it take to build a website?

The timeline varies depending on the complexity of the project. A basic website can be completed in 2-4 weeks, while more complex sites with custom functionality may take 8-12 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

What is your payment structure?

We typically require a 50% deposit to begin work, with the remaining balance due upon project completion. For larger projects, we may establish a payment schedule with milestones throughout the development process.
